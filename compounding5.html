Simple Rules

1. Every player starts with $10,000 balance and $1,000 compounded wealth.
2. Each turn shows 2 investment cards and 2 loan cards.
3. Shuffle selects exactly 1 card.
4. Investment cards increase compounded wealth.
5. Loan cards reduce balance and add expense drag.
6. Home loan cards cannot be skipped and create a growing home asset.
