Budget Boss Multiplayer

Play in 1, 2, or 4 player mode. Roll the dice, answer money questions, earn points, and learn smart budgeting through turn-based play.

Rules

1. Choose 1, 2, or 4 players and enter player names.

2. The current player rolls the dice and moves on the board.

3. A money question appears. Pick the best answer before time runs out.

4. Faster answers earn extra points.

5. Every player has separate cash, savings, score, and board position.

6. After all rounds are complete, the player with the highest score wins.
