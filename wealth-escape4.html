Rules

🎯 Main Goal

Win by making your Passive Income ≥ Expenses. The first non-bankrupt player to do this wins.

💵 Monthly Cashflow

Monthly cashflow is:
Salary + Passive Income - Expenses - Debt Cost

💥 Bankruptcy

If a player's cash goes below -$1000, that player is bankrupt and out of the game.

🏠 Deals & Assets

Buy deals only when you have enough cash. Assets reduce short-term cash but increase long-term passive income.

🎲 Turn Flow

On your turn: roll dice, move, handle the tile, buy or skip deals, then end your turn.
